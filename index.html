Follow these steps to create your volunteer roster calendar:

  1. Input your SAKG session information into the SAKG classes sheet
  2. Send your 'Request for SAKG volunteer' messages via Geddup
  3. Copy and paste your volunteer data directly from your Geddup export files into the Volunteers sheet
  4. Click Add data to push your sessions to the Raw Data sheet
  5. Click Create calendar to push all sessions and volunteers to your Google calendar
Once you have 'Added' your classes, we suggest that you manage any changes from within the calendar itself.

You can always re-open these instructions by clicking on Getting Started again.
